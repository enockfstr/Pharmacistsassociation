  <section id="strategy">
    <h2>Strategic Plan Highlights</h2>
    <p>We are aligned with Malawi’s MW2063 vision and health strategic pillars. Our focus includes sector-wide collaboration, governance transparency, CPD integration, and health systems strengthening.</p>
    <h3>SWOT Analysis:</h3>
    <p><strong>Strengths:</strong> Registered status, unified purpose, ethical framework.</p>
    <p><strong>Weaknesses:</strong> Pending emblem approval, early-stage operations.</p>
    <p><strong>Opportunities:</strong> National health alignment, CPD recognition, strategic partnerships.</p>
    <p><strong>Threats:</strong> Limited resources, external dependency.</p>
  </section>
